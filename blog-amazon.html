RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM: recommendation system is an extensive class of web applications that involve predicting user responses to options.

Recommendation systems help users discover content based on their unique needs and preferences. So it's all about customization and making it tailored to a person's needs and preferences. #A recommendation system learns from a customer and recommends products or information that he/she will find most valuable. The Recommender System is what businesses need in many ways and brings up to 30% of additional revenue to businesses.

Recommendation systems are a type of filter to narrow down a list of available options and help users find relevant items within huge available thing to selection.

There are three types of recommendations

1. Collaborative filtering

  • It recommends items based on the interests of a community of users.

2. Content-Based filtering

  • Recommend items with similar content.

3. Hybrid Content-Based Collaborative filtering

  • Combination of the two above approaches to overcome the disadvantages of each approach
